## Reporting a Vulnerability

We take the security of `pypss` seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please do **not** report it as a public issue on GitHub.

### How to Report

Please email the project maintainers directly at **dattateja44@gmail.com**.

Include the following details in your report:

*   **Type of Issue:** (e.g., buffer overflow, SQL injection, cross-site scripting, etc.)
*   **Full paths of source file(s) related to the manifestation of the issue.**
*   **The location of the affected source code:** (tag/branch/commit or direct URL).
*   **Any special configuration required to reproduce the issue.**
*   **Step-by-step instructions to reproduce the issue.**
*   **Proof-of-concept or exploit code** (if available).
*   **Impact of the issue** (e.g., how an attacker might exploit it).

### Response Timeline

*   We will acknowledge your report within **48 hours**.
*   We will provide a status update or a fix estimate within **1 week**.
*   We will coordinate the public release of the fix and security advisory with you.

### Bounty

Currently, `pypss` is an open-source project maintained by volunteers and does not offer a monetary bug bounty program. However, we will happily acknowledge your contribution in our release notes and Hall of Fame (with your permission).
